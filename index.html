Traditional pizza varieties always contain meat products. For a milder taste, use chicken meat, for piquancy - spicy sausage, most often salami or pepperoni. Other commonly used ingredients are ham, bacon, and boiled meat. The “home-style” meat filling can consist of minced meat, boiled sausage or sausages.
